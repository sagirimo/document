# week 6

# supremacy

霸权，至高无上；优势

the state or condition of being superior to all others in authority, power, or status

# transgression

犯罪；违反

an act that goes against a law, rule, or code of conduct. In the Bible, it means a breach of command, offense, crime, or sin

# delve

耕；钻研；探究；挖

dig

# spin

织；旋转

draw out (wool, cotton, or other material) and convert it into threads, either by hand or with machinery

# eternity

无穷无尽的时间；永恒，永久；永生；未来，来世；死

ever, all time

# parody

滑稽模仿作品（文章、音乐作品或表演的滑稽仿作等）；拙劣的模仿，荒诞不经的事

an imitation of the style of a particular writer, artist, or genre with deliberate exaggeration for comic effect

# irony

反语，讽刺

the expression of one's meaning by using language that normally signifies the opposite, typically for humorous or emphatic effect

# epitaph

碑文，墓志铭

a phrase or statement written in memory of a person who has died, especially as an inscription on a tombstone.

# epigram

警句；讽刺短诗；隽语

a pithy saying or remark expressing an idea in a clever and amusing way

# fowl

家禽；鸟；飞禽

any domesticated bird

# lodge

（使）固定，（使）卡住；寄存

make or become firmly fixed or embedded in a place

# Sabbath

安息日

a day of religious observance and abstinence from work, kept by Jewish people from Friday evening to Saturday evening, and by most Christians on Sunday

# prowl

潜行；徘徊；搜寻

(of a person or animal) move about restlessly and stealthily, especially in search of prey

# justify

是……的正当理由；对……作出解释，为……辩解；使（文本）对齐；（在上帝的眼中）称义，使有义；证明合法

show or prove to be right or reasonable

# status quo

现状（the status quo）

the existing state of affairs, especially regarding social or political issues
