# week 2

# Christianize

（使）皈依基督教；（使）基督教化

make somesone Christian; convert to Christianity

# antiquity

古时（尤指古埃及、古希腊、古罗马时期）

the ancient past, esp. the period before the Middle Ages

# rigid

刚硬的，不易弯曲的

stiff and unmoving

# mortality

死亡；死亡人数，死亡率；必死性，终有一死

death, especially on a large scale

# bronze

青铜

a yellowish-brown alloy of copper with up to one-third tin

# nude

裸体的

a naked human figure, typically as the subject of a painting, sculpture, or photograph

# paraphrase

释义，改述（同语言）

express the meaning of using different words, especially to achieve greater clarity

# Armageddon

大决战；世界末日善恶决战的战场（源于《圣经》）

(in the New Testament) the last battle between good and evil before the Day of Judgment.

# secular

非宗教的，世俗的

denoting（指示）attitudes, activities, or other things that have no religious or spiritual basis

# reason

理性

the power of the mind to think, understand, and form judgments by a process of logic:

# infinite

无限的

Limitless, boundless

# faculty

天赋；才能

talent

# express

高速运行的

operating at high speed

# apprehension

理解

understanding

# paragon

完美典范，尽善尽美的人（或物）

a person or thing regarded as a perfect example of a particular quality
