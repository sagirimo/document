# week 7

# archer

弓手

a person who shoots with a bow and arrows

# vault

拱顶，穹隆

a roof in the form of an arch or a series of arches

# alternate

（使）交替，（使）轮流

occur in turn repeatedly

# monarch

君主，女王

a sovereign（君主，元首）head of state, especially a king, queen, or emperor

# patriarch

（男性）家长，族长；德高望重的男长者；（基督教东派教会的）大牧首，宗主教（Patriarch）

the male head of a family or tribe; any of those biblical figures regarded as fathers of the human race, especially Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob, and their forefathers, or the sons of Jacob.

# deluge

暴雨，洪水

a severe flood

# chronological

按发生时间顺序排列的；（年龄）按时间计算的

(of a record of events) starting with the earliest and following the order in which they occurred:

# replicate

重复，复制

make an exact copy of

# hospitality

殷勤款待，好客

the friendly and generous reception（接待）and entertainment of guests, visitors, or strangers

# brimstone

硫磺

Sulfur（硫，硫磺）

# sovereignty

主权，最高统治权，最高权威；独立自主；主权国家，自治州

supreme power or authority

# covenant

（上帝与人所立的）约；盟约，契约；协议，盖印合同（尤指向慈善机构定期捐款）

an agreement which brings about a relationship of commitment between God and his people

# chapel

小礼拜堂，小教堂；礼拜

a small building for Christian worship, typically one attached to an institution or private house
