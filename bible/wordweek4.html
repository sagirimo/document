# week 4

# halo

光环

a disk or circle of light shown surrounding or above the head of a saint or holy person to represent their holiness.

# emblem

纹章

a thing serving as a symbolic representation of a particular quality or concept

# apostle/disciple

（耶稣的十二个）门徒

each of the twelve chief disciples of Jesus Christ/ a personal follower of Jesus during his life, especially one of the twelve Apostles

# patron

庇护人；赞助人

the protecting or guiding saint of a person or place.

# arch

拱

a curved symmetrical structure spanning an opening

# triumph

巨大成功，重大胜利

a great victory or achievement

# contemporary

当代的，现代的；同时期的，同时代的

a person or thing living or existing at the same time as another

# armor

盔甲，甲胄

protecting covering

# banner

军旗；横幅标语；（网页上的）横幅广告；信仰，准则；旗帜

a flag on a pole used as the standard of a monarch, army, or knight

# convert

（使）改变信仰，（使）皈依；

change one's religious faith or other beliefs

# pope

教皇，罗马教皇

the Bishop of Rome as head of the Roman Catholic Church.

# prototype

原型

a first, typical or preliminary model of something, especially a machine, from which other forms are developed or copied
