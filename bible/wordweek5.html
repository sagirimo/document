# week 5

# angelology

天使学

theological dogma or speculation concerning angels

# cherubim

小天使（cherub 的复数）

a representation of an angelic being in art, depicted as a chubby, healthy-looking child with wings

# seraphim

六翼天使（seraph 的复数）

an angelic being, regarded in traditional Christian angelology as belonging to the highest order of the ninefold celestial hierarchy

# archangel

大天使；天使长；第八级天使

an angel of high rank (in traditional Christian angelology, a being of the eighth order of the ninefold celestial hierarchy)

# Satan

撒旦（魔鬼）

from Hebrew śāṭān, literally ‘adversary’

# ignorance

无知，愚昧

lack of knowledge or information

# exalt

提升，提拔；颂扬，吹捧；使得意

raise to a higher rank or a position of greater power

# speculation

猜测，推测，推断

the forming of a theory without firm evidence

# annunciation

天使传报；圣母领报节；天使报喜画；告示

the announcement of the incarnation by the angel Gabriel to Mary (Luke 1:26–38)

# opaque

（玻璃、液体等）不透明的，不透光的；难懂的，隐晦的；迟钝的

not able to be seen through; not transparent

# inferno

阴间，地狱

(usually Inferno) hell (with reference to Dante's Divine Comedy).

# melancholy

（令人）悲哀的，（令人）沮丧的

sad and pensive

# purgatory

炼狱；涤罪；暂时的苦难

(in Roman Catholic doctrine) a place or state of suffering inhabited by the souls of sinners who are expiating their sins before going toheaven
