# week12

# restitution

恢复；赔偿；归还

recompense for injury or loss

# tabernacle

神龛；礼拜堂，会堂；圣所，会幕；临时房屋；帐篷；桅座，夹桅板；圣体盒

a tent used as a sanctuary for the Ark of the Covenant by the Israelites during the Exodus and until the building of the Temple

# eclipse

日食，月食；黯然失色；羽毛暗淡期；消失，丧失

an occasion when the sun disappears from view, either completely or partly, while the moon is moving between it and the earth, or when the moon becomes darker while the shadow of the earth moves over it

# bereave

使丧失亲友；剥夺

Deprive（剥夺，使丧失；免去……的职务（尤指圣职））

# prime

首要的；

First in numerical order

# decree

法令，政令；裁定，判决

An official order

# desert

离弃，舍弃（某地）；抛弃，遗弃（某人）；背弃，放弃；擅离，开小差；突然丧失

abandon

# quench

（喝水）止（渴）；满足（欲望）；扑灭，熄灭（火）；抑制，压制（感情）；（尤指用冷水或油）将……淬火，使冷却；控制（发光、震动或放电等效果）

Extinguish（熄灭；消灭，使破灭；<法律>取消（权利，义务），使无效；偿清；压制）

# diffuse

扩散，弥漫；减弱，平息（不良情绪或局面）；传播，散布

spread or cause to spread over a wide area or among a large number of people

# exile

流放；使离开

Expel（驱逐）and bar（阻止，禁止；阻挡，拦住；封，堵）from their native country

# foe

敌人，对头

An enemy or opponent
