# Example:two versions of Virgin of the Rocks

(p-b-p structure)

these two paintings are so alike that its hard to tell them apart.

but, if we study these two paintings more carefully, we can discover the differ in compositional details,in color and in tone.

Compositionally, all the figures are a bit bigger in the right one than in the left painting. The main compositional difference is while in the right painting, the angel rests the hand, the left one's angel's is rised. The index finger pointing at John.

The eyes of the angel turn down in the left painting, but in the right painting, the eyes turn to gaze in the general direction of the viewer.

The right one contains traditional attributes missing from the left version. For example, the halo and crossing form staff.

the right version is softer and smoother.It's the result of tone.

The contrast in the light and shape on the figures and faces in the right painting, are all much sharper.

The faces and forms in the left one are delicatedly painted.

Conclusion:

These differences make the two versions as secret as famous
