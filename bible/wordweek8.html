# cling

抓住，抱紧；黏附，粘住

hold on tightly to

# dangle

悬荡

hang or swing loosely

# ventilation

通风，通风系统

the provision of fresh air to a room, building

# horizontal

水平的

parallel to the plane of the horizon

# vertical

垂直的

in a direction, or having an alignment, such that the top is directly above the bottom

# diagonal

对角线的，对角的；斜的，斜线的；沿斜线运动的

joining two opposite corners of a square, rectangle, or other straight-sided shape

# intersect

相交，交叉

divide(something)by passing or lying across it

# husbandry

饲养业，农牧业

the care, cultivation, and breeding of crops and animals

# vineyard

葡萄园；葡萄酒厂

a plantation of grapevines, typically producing grapes used in winemaking

# age

变老

grow old or older, especially visibly and obviously so

# bond

纽带

a relationship between people or groups based on shared feelings, interests, or experiences

# abstinence

节制；节欲；戒酒；禁食

the fact or practice of restraining（克制）oneself from indulging（沉迷；纵容，放纵）in something
