# week10

# wonder

奇观，奇迹；令人惊讶的事物，令人难以置信的事物

(in “work/do wonder”) spectacularly fine results

# miracle

奇迹，不可思议的事；杰出例子，精品

a surprising and welcome event that is not explicable by natural or scientific laws and is therefore considered to be the work of a divine

# analogy

类比，比拟；用类比方法，进行比照；同功

a comparison between two things, typically for the purpose of explanation or clarification

# protagonist

主人公

the leading character or one of the major characters in a drama, movie, novel, or other fictional text

# antagonist

对手，敌手，对立者；拮抗物；拮抗肌

a person who actively opposes or is hostile to someone or something; an adversary（敌手，对手）

# literal

字面上的；逐字的；缺乏想象力的，刻板的；确切的，不夸张的；（图像）写实的；（用）字母（表示）的

taking words in their usual or most basic sense

# metaphoric

隐喻的

characteristic of or relating to metaphor（隐喻，暗喻；象征，标志）; figurative（比喻的；形象的；有文采的，用修辞手段的）

# induction

归纳，归纳法

the inference（推断，推理；推断结果，结论）of a general law from particular instances

# deduction

推论，演绎法

the inference（推断，推理；推断结果，结论）of particular instances by reference（根据）to a general law or principle

# consecrate

奉献；使神圣；供神用；献身于

make or declare（宣布）(something, typically a church) sacred（神的，神圣的；宗教的，宗教性的；受尊重的，受崇敬的；不可侵犯的，不容干涉的）; dedicate（致力于，献身于）formally to a religious or divine（神的）purpose

# Eucharist

圣餐

the Christian service, ceremony, or sacrament commemorating the Last Supper, in which bread and wine are consecrated and consumed
