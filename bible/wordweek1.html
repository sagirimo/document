# week 1

# interdisciplinary

跨学科的

relating to more than one branch of knowledge

# sanctity

圣洁性；尊严；神圣不可侵犯性

the state or quality of being holy, sacred, or saintly

# verse

（《圣经》或其他经文的）节

each of the short numbered divisions of a chapter in the Bible or other scripture

# motif

主题；主旨；图形；意念；基序，模体

theme

# gist

要点，主旨，大意；<法律>诉讼要点

the essence of a speech or text

# allusion

暗指，影射，典故

an expression designed to call something to mind without mentioning it explicitly; an indirect or passing reference

# subvocalize

默读

utter (words or sounds) with the lips silently or with barely audible sound, especially when talking to oneself, memorizing something, or reading

# skim

略读，浏览

read (something) quickly or cursorily so as to note only the important points

# testament

（基督教的）圣约书（Testament）

(in biblical use) a covenant

# biblical

《圣经》中的，有关《圣经》的

relating to or contained in the Bible

# Behemoth

巨兽；河马

a huge or monstrous creature

# swine

猪猡

a pig

# gird

束缚；佩带

secure (a garment or sword) on the body with a belt or band

# loin

腰；腰部；腰肉

the part of the body on both sides of the spine between the lowest (false) ribs and the hipbones

# tunic

（古希腊古罗马人穿，长度及膝的）无袖外衣；（罩在裙子和裤子外）宽松外衣；紧身上衣

a loose garment, typically sleeveless and reaching to the wearer's knees
