# spotlight

聚光灯，反光灯；媒体和公众的注意；聚光灯照明圈

a lamp projecting a narrow, intense beam of light directly on to a place or person, especially a performer on stage

# gouge

用锐利工具/刀片挖用半圆凿子挖；欺骗

make a hole with or as with a sharp tool or blade

# pagan

异教徒（信非主流宗教者）；非基督教徒；新异教主义者；无宗教信仰者

a person holding religious beliefs other than those of the main world religions

# vow

誓约，诺言；（婚礼等上的）誓言

a solemn promise

# beehive

蜂窝；蜂箱

a dome-shaped or boxlike structure in which bees are kept

# flaw

削弱，使失效；缺点，缺陷；裂痕，瑕疵

Fault or weakness in a person's character

# dialect

方言，土话

a particular form of a language which is peculiar to a specific region or social group

# convention

习俗，惯例；大会，集会；公约，协定；（文学、艺术上的）传统手法，传统风格

a way in which something is usually done, especially within a particular area or activity

# seductive

有魅力的，性感的；有吸引力的，令人神往的

tempting and attractive

# treacherous

（人）不忠的，背信弃义的；（地面、道路、天气情况等）暗藏危险的

A guilty of or involving betrayal or deception
