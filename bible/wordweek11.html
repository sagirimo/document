# week11

# reveal

揭示，透露；表明，证明；展示，显示；（通过神或超自然手段）启示

make (previously unknown or secret information) known to others

# sparingly

保守的，一点点，俭省地

in a restricted or infrequent manner; in small quantities

# impoverished

贫困的，赤贫的；

poor

# devout

虔诚的；衷心的

having or showing deep religious feeling or commitment

# tempestuous

有暴风雨的；暴乱的；剧烈的

characterized by strong and turbulent（骚乱的，动乱的）or conflicting emotion

# fiery

易怒的，暴躁的；怒气冲冲的，充满激情的；

(of a person) having a passionate, quick-tempered nature

# exceptional

卓越的，杰出的；不寻常的，罕见的；（心理或身体上）有缺陷的

unusually good; outstanding

# microcosm

微观世界；小宇宙；作为宇宙缩影的人类；缩图（等于 microcosmos）

a community, place, or situation regarded as encapsulating（封装；封进内部）in miniature the characteristic qualities or features of something much larger

# drapery

布料；帏帐；打褶的帐幔

cloth, curtains, or clothing hanging in loose folds

# idolatry

偶像崇拜；盲目崇拜；邪神崇拜

the worship of idols

# testimony

（尤指法庭上的）证词，证言；证据，证明；（宗教皈依或经历的）公开表白

a formal written or spoken statement, especially one given in a court of law

# adultery

通奸，通奸行为

voluntary sexual intercourse（交际，沟通；性交，交媾）between a married person and a person who is not his or her spouse（配偶）

# perjury

伪证；伪誓；背信弃义

the offense（违法，犯罪；冒犯，伤害；过错，不当）of willfully telling an untruth in a court after having taken an oath（宣誓）or affirmation（断言）
