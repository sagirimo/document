# week9

# relief

浮雕，浮雕品

in sculpture, any work in which the figures project from a supporting background

# deception

欺骗，蒙骗；骗术，骗局

in the act of causing someone to believe something that is not true

# oracle

神谕，神示；

a message from God

# ascend

升职，晋升；上升，升高，通往高处；追溯，溯源；攀登，登上

go up

# descend

下来，下降；下倾，下斜

move downward

# chronicle

编年史，大事记；（报纸名称）记事报；记事，叙述

a factual written account of important or historical events in the order of their occurrence

# prophet

先知；预言家；提倡者，拥护者

a person regarded as an inspired teacher or proclaimer of the will of God

# octangular

八角的，八角形的

having eight angles

# octagonal

八边形的

eight-sided

刻板印象

a widely held but fixed and oversimplified image or idea of a particular type of person or thing:
