# week 3

# humanities

人文学科（复数）

learning or literature concerned with human culture, especially literature, history, art, music, and philosophy.

# nude

裸体的

a naked human figure as the subject of a painting, sculpture, or photograph

# witness

见证（者）

a person who is present at the signing of a document and signs it themselves to confirm this

# redemption

（基督教指耶稣的）救赎，赎罪；（从过失或罪恶中）拯救人的事物

the action of saving or being saved from sin, error, or evil

# Catholic

罗马天主教的

relating to the historic doctrine and practice of the Western Church

# Orthodox

东正教派的；正教的

relating to the historic doctrine and practice of the Eastern Church

# heresy

异端；异端邪说；异教

belief or opinion contrary to orthodox religious doctrine

# heretical

异端的；异教的

believing in or practicing religious heresy

# sect

宗派

a group of people with somewhat different religious beliefs from those of a larger group to which they belong.

# persecute

（尤指因种族、宗教或政治信仰而）迫害

subject (someone) to hostility and ill-treatment, especially because of their race or political or religious beliefs

# polytheism

多神论；多神教

the belief in or worship of more than one god.

# monotheism

一神教，一神论

the doctrine or belief that there is only one God.

# atheism

不信神，无神论

disbelief or lack of belief in the existence of God or gods.

# schism

分裂

a split or division between strongly opposed sections or parties, caused by differences in opinion or belief.
